July 9, 2020

Olentangy High School's HOSA places at International Leadership Conference

Featuring: HOSA

Olentangy High School's HOSA placed multiple times at this year's virtual International Leadership Conference. Min-Song Kim, Rachel Matthew, and Vishal Vivek placed first in their team's event: Community Awareness. Their team decided to focus on HIV/AIDS and reducing the sigma surrounding it. This marks the first time OHS HOSA has placed first at the international level. Min-Song Kim also won third place in the Academic Testing Center for Biology.

Abigail Swamidoss, Vidhya Kasiraman, Yadnya Sonawane, Naina Karnati, and Archita Sriram placed second internationally in their event: MRC Partnership. The team focused this year on establishing, growing, and maintaining a partnership with the Delaware MRC (Medical Reserve Corps).

Samhith Kethireddy, Aashna Iragamreddy, and Isha Sawhney placed third internationally in their event: Medical Innovation. Their team designed a 3D printed lower extremity exoskeleton that allows patients with paraplegia to experience touch sensation.

Congratulations to each team for placing internationally! More information regarding each team can be found on the Ohio HOSA website under the Summer 2020 Newsletter.
