Our Mission

Hi! We are Abigail Swamidoss, Samhith Kethireddy, and Min-Song Kim. We are students at Olentangy High School and we noticed a lack of an effective and convenient platform for students to explore and recognize the various clubs offered at Olentangy. We designed and created this website to solve this issue. Thank you for visiting our website!

About Our Website

To explore our website, simply select the school you attend under the "Schools" tab on the navigation bar above. You will be provided with a list of clubs offered at your school. These lists are not exhaustive and clubs may still exist at your school even though they are not listed on our website. Feel free to also explore the "Recognition" tab which posts awards that clubs have won.

Changes & Edits

If you are a member or leader of a club and want to update, add, remove, or edit any information provided on our website simply locate the "Contact & Update" tab on the navigation bar or email us at olentangyclubdirectory@gmail.com. We will update this information as soon as possible. You can also contact us with the additional information provided on the "Contact & Update" page.
